Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter while using the app.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and spending breakdowns.
  • To improve the app based on general usage patterns.

We will never sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on a secured server. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or expense data with any third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface — this involves a request to Google's servers but does not include any of your personal or financial data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
